RATAN PEARLS

Ratan Pearls is a residential apartment complex developed by Ratan Housing / Ratan Buildtech in GH‑01D, Sector‑16, Greater Noida West. The development sits on about 4.26 acres of land, with four towers and roughly 588 units. Apartment sizes include 2‑BHK and 3‑BHK layouts, with the smaller units starting at around 1,035 sq ft, and larger ones up to about 1,874 sq ft. The project is RERA‑registered under IDs UPRERAPRJ8629 and UPRERAPRJ15192.

RATAN PEARLS

What sets Ratan Pearls apart is its strong emphasis on open, green space and thoughtful architectural design. About 85% of the site is devoted to landscaped gardens, open areas, and amenities, leaving only ~15% as built‑up area. The towers are designed to allow 360° openness so that many units get natural light and cross‑ventilation. The project includes green features such as rainwater harvesting, energy‑efficient lighting, and an RCC frame structure that offers earthquake resistance.

RATAN PEARLS

In terms of amenities, connectivity, and status, Ratan Pearls offers a comfortable modern lifestyle. Amenities include a clubhouse, gym, swimming pool, landscaped gardens, indoor/outdoor sports courts, jogging track, senior citizens’ zone, children’s play areas, etc. The project is ready‑to‑move in many parts, having possession phases like Phase II reaching completion around December 2023. It is reasonably well connected with access to major roads, nearby schools, hospitals, and shopping, typical of Greater Noida West.
