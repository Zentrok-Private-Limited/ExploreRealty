About

IBP Windsor Valley in Noida Extension is a premium residential project offering modern living in a well-planned gated community. Located in Sector 16B, Greater Noida West, the project features well-designed 2 and 3 BHK independent villas and floors with spacious layouts, quality construction, and essential amenities like 24×7 security, landscaped gardens, street lighting, and a proposed clubhouse. With its strategic location, proximity to daily conveniences, and a focus on both comfort and lifestyle, IBP Windsor Valley is an ideal choice for families seeking a peaceful yet connected urban living experience.
