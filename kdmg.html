What is Central Plaza

Central Plaza is a commercial retail / high‑street project located in West Side, Greater Noida West (also known as Greater Noida, Sector‑4 / Techzone type area). It’s designed as a multi‑storey shopping / business destination with retail shops, food court / multi‑cuisine restaurants, showrooms, and related commercial amenity spaces. It aims to serve both as a consumer destination (shopping, food, entertainment) and as an investment opportunity (shops for rent or sale).

Key Features & Specs

• The total plot size is approximately 3,000 square metres. Rera Prop

• The development has high street retail shops from Lower Ground Floor (LGF) up to 2nd Floor, plus dedicated floors for restaurants and food court (restaurants on 3rd Floor, food court on 4th) in the RERA filing. Rera Prop

• Minimum shop size is about 168 sq. ft. Rera Prop

• Facilities include covered car parking for 250+ vehicles. Rera Prop

• The tower height is LG + 6 floors.

Investment / Financial Highlights

• Shops are being offered with guaranteed lease rates by floor:

• Lower Ground Floor (LGF): approx. ₹65 per square foot

• Ground Floor: approx. ₹132 per square foot

• First Floor: approx. ₹75 per square foot

• Second Floor: approx. ₹60 per square foot Rera Prop

• There is a claim that there will be ~15% appreciation every 3 years. Rera Prop

• Also, GST input credit is made available to buyers.
