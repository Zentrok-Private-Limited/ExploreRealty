AU Silicon City

AU Silicon City was initially a part of the Amrapali Group's extensive housing plans, but they were put on hold because of financial irregularities and poor management. Thousands of homebuyers were left in limbo by these incomplete projects, which caused widespread annoyance and prompted legal action.

AU Silicon City

In a historic ruling, the Supreme Court of India charged NBCC (India) Ltd., a Navratna Central Public Sector Enterprise under the Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs (MoHUA), with carrying out these housing projects in a manner overseen by the court. As a result, ASPIRE (Amrapali Stalled Projects Investments Reconstruction Establishment), a Section 8 business, was established to manage the construction, repair, and delivery of impacted properties, including NBCC Silicon City.

AU Silicon City

More than just a housing development, NBCC Silicon City is a tale of rebirth, a dedication to openness, effectiveness, and providing promised homes to their rightful owners. In order to meet the needs of today's urban families, the development includes a variety of mid- to high-rise residential towers, green infrastructure, and contemporary amenities.
