TULSI VATIKA VILLAS
Tulsi Vatika Royal Villas is a premium villa project located in Vaidpura, Sector-10, Greater Noida West. It offers spacious 3 BHK villas ranging from approximately 1,872 to 2,085 sq. ft., with prices starting around ₹97 lakh and going up to ₹1.08 crore.
The project features 44 units and promises amenities like a clubhouse, landscaped gardens, temple, and security. Currently under construction, it is expected to be completed by December 2025. Positioned near Rajesh Pilot Chowk, it offers decent connectivity and is marketed as a blend of comfort, privacy, and modern living.
