Ivory County
Ivory County is a premium residential project by County Group located in Sector 115, Noida. Spread across approximately 28 acres, it offers ultra-luxury 3, 4, and 5 BHK apartments with world-class amenities and elegant design.
The project is RERA-registered and features spacious layouts, high-end finishes, and a host of lifestyle facilities like a clubhouse, swimming pool, and landscaped gardens. Strategically situated near major expressways and metro connectivity, Ivory County promises a blend of comfort, luxury, and modern urban living.
