Corbett County

Corbett County is a premium plotted and villa development located in Bail Parao, Ramnagar (Uttarakhand), near Jim Corbett National Park. Spread across approx 15–18 acres, the project offers a perfect blend of nature and luxury living. It features residential plots and independent villas, ideal for holiday homes, second homes, and investment purposes. The project is RERA registered, ensuring transparency and security.

Corbett County

Designed as a low-density, nature-inspired community, Corbett County focuses on open green spaces and peaceful surroundings. Unlike crowded high-rise developments, this project offers wide internal roads, landscaped gardens, and forest-facing plots. It provides a calm and pollution-free environment, making it perfect for weekend getaways and long-term living.

Corbett County

The project offers resort-style amenities including a clubhouse, swimming pool, gym, yoga & meditation zones, kids play area, amphitheatre, and nature trails. Unique features like horse riding and hospitality services enhance the lifestyle experience. Due to its proximity to Jim Corbett Park, it also offers strong rental income potential through vacation stays.
